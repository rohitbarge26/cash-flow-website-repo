Project Finance

Fundraising for long tenure Projects requires in-depth industry know-how, financial expertise, and extensive risk management experience to ensure stakeholders benefit from investment for critical projects. Cashflow Solutions specializes in project finance across sectors like Real Estate, Manufacturing, Infrastructure, Gems and Jewellery, Service Sector etc.

Working Capital

Effective working capital management is crucial to the success of any business. From revolving lines of credit to trade-finance facilities (Buyers' Credit) and invoice financing or non-Fund-based facilities like Bank Guarantees and Letter of Credit, working capital solutions from Cashflow Solutions is designed to provide flexibility and cost-effectiveness within a stable, long-term partnership.

Term Loan / Loan Against Property / Lease Rental Discounting / Inventory Funding

Cashflow Solutions assists in fundraising for capital expenditure and expansion of businesses in the form of a term loan, LAP, LRD, Inventory Funding for real estate, etc. It enables to provide tailor made products to suit the various financial needs of the business. We focus on providing adequate solutions to businesses to accommodate their requirements.

Particularly for products like LAP, LRD & Inventory funding, the eligibility criteria and interest rate widely vary from bank to bank. Our team keeps updated on all the products of all the major banks, so as to facilitate the client to provide the best possible terms.
