Our Products

Explore our comprehensive suite of financial products designed to support your business growth and capital needs.

SME & Working Capital Loans

Inconsistent cash flows can disrupt daily operations and stall commercial expansion. Cashflow Solutions supports small and medium enterprises (SMEs) by identifying appropriate institutional lenders, handling complex legal documentation, and securing timely, hassle-free disbursement. Manage short-term financial frameworks, payroll, or supplier cycles without collateral.

Includes:
  • Cash Credit
  • Overdraft
  • Bank Guarantee
  • Letter of Credit
  • Term Loan
  • Bills Discounting
  • Loans against Import/Exports

Project Finance

Fundraising for long-tenure enterprise projects requires deep industry domain knowledge, financial engineering capabilities, and robust risk management structures. Cashflow Solutions specializes in complex project financing across key growth sectors including Real Estate, Manufacturing, Infrastructure, Gems & Jewellery, and the Service Industry.

Loan against Property (LAP)

Unlock the latent financial power of your residential, commercial, or industrial land assets without liquidating them. Cashflow Solutions streamlines the asset evaluation process, compares interest rates across top-tier institutions, and ensures transparent terms to secure large-scale funding for commercial growth or emergency requirements.

Business Loans

Address sudden market fluctuations or invest in immediate plant and machinery upgrades with unsecured business credits. Tailored to manage foundational start-up rollouts, business footprint expansions, or sudden working capital needs at competitive interest structures.

Lease Rental Discounting (LRD)

Access corporate capital quickly without exhausting primary collateral. Cashflow Solutions structures LRD setups by discounting future verified rental income receipts from corporate properties, enabling businesses to unlock immediate long-term lump-sum funds.

Home Loans

Navigate homeownership options seamlessly across top banks and NBFCs. We optimize the home loan acquisition process based on your unique credit history, income profiles, and location choices, offering highly competitive secured interest rates.
